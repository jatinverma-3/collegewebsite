About Us

We are the world's largest university

Ness Wadia College of Commerce, situated in the heart of Pune, stands as one of the leading institutions for commerce education in India. Established in 1969, this college has carved a niche for itself by offering quality education that blends academic rigor with practical insights. Affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University, the college has earned a reputation for excellence in commerce and related fields.
