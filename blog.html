Our Certificate & online programs for 2023

Ness Wadia College of Commerce, situated in the heart of Pune, stands as one of the leading institutions for commerce education in India. Established in 1969, this college has carved a niche for itself by offering quality education that blends academic rigor with practical insights. Affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University, the college has earned a reputation for excellence in commerce and related fields.


The college offers a wide range of programs, including undergraduate degrees like B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce), BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration), and BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications), as well as postgraduate courses like M.Com (Master of Commerce). Additionally, it offers professional certification courses in Chartered Accountancy (CA), Company Secretary (CS), and Cost and Management Accountancy (CMA), equipping students for the competitive corporate world.


The campus is well-equipped with modern infrastructure, including digital classrooms, a well-stocked library, sports facilities, and a dedicated placement cell that works tirelessly to provide students with career opportunities. The faculty comprises highly qualified professionals, many of whom are industry experts, ensuring that students receive both theoretical knowledge and practical exposure.


In conclusion, Ness Wadia College of Commerce is a beacon of commerce education in Pune, offering a well-rounded, future-ready education that empowers students to excel in the global business landscape.
