Introduction :
Programme is designed to impart business and management skills to aspiring students for a better understanding of the corporate world and its business activities. It would help students plan their business activity properly in various capacities. Some of the popular electives available in the college are - Banking and Finance, Business administration, Marketing, Cost and works accounting, Business Entrepreneurship, Business Statistics
Admission Procedure
We are pleased that you have decided to apply for admission to the Ness Wadia College of Commerce, Pune. The college accepts a variety of applications for various programs, so make sure that you select the one that works best for you.
STEPS FOR ADMISSION
Filling Of Merit Form.
Upload required documents as specified in the Merit Form.
Merit List will be prepared based on Merit and reservation and will be uploaded on website.
Await the notice for Admission Form.
Admission will be confirmed only after payment of fees within mentioned time span.
Merit list for next round will be put up in case seats are available.
Eligibility for students : From all streams ( Arts , Commerce, & Science)>40% can apply.
Please note that the entire process is now online.
