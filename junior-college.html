Junior College
Introduction :
The Junior College engages in imparting the knowledge of Commerce subjects to students on the basis of guidelines prescribed by the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education. Std XI is the first of the two years that comprise the +2 level of education.
Steps for Std.XI Online College Admission
Students should click “Proceed for Admission” on – “pune.11thadmission.org.in” if okay with allotted Jr.college
Visit College website- https://nwcc.mespune.org/ for admission details.
Click on ness.vriddhionline.com to fill admission form for Std.XI Commerce Aided(Grant)/Un-aided(Non Grant) depending upon the type you have been admitted in the Std XI Centralised Online Admission Process.
The student will have to select “STUDENT LOGIN” and then” STUDENT REGISTER” on the main page of the above website and register first by using their Mobile number and email id.
After the registration is complete, you will receive a “Student Registration ID” and the “Password” you have created. (You have to keep this for your future login to the above vriddhi college website to fill and update your college form if and when required.)
Students have to login in to “Students Login” again on the Vriddhi college website main page available to fill the admission form. Once you login with your Student’s Reg ID and password, it will direct you to the instructions. Read the instructions carefully and proceed to the “Dashboard” where you will find an option “Admission” on it. Select “Fill Form” option and proceed to fill the form.
Pay charges online for Brochure code (Vriddhi Site) and save the code & proceed to fill the form.
It is mandatory for the students to save their completed admission form and take printout on A4 Size green paper. Please bring this printout to the college to continue with admission procedure.
While filling the first page, students should select their Section carefully- - Jr.Grant for Aided and Jr.Non Grant for Un-aided (as per allotment)in Centralised Online Admission list without this the students will not be able to fill the college admission form.
Students are expected to fill the admission form completely and upload necessary documents :
Scanned copy of 10th Marksheet
 Scanned copy of 10th School Leaving Certificate if received.
Scanned copy of students Caste Certificate only (if applicable)
 Scanned copy of Special Category-Sports, Ex-serviceman, Physical Handicapped certificate (if applicable)
Undertaking (if applicable)
Students from other boards (except SSC state board) should also upload their scanned copy of Migration Certificate if they have received it on order to start their Eligibility Process.
All Students should fill up an “Undertaking Form” specifying their responsibility of submitting all original documents like School Leaving Certificate, Caste Certificate etc. whichever necessary and applicable with respect to eligibility.
Once the fees are paid the online admission process will be completed.
It is mandatory for the students to save their completed admission form and take printout on A4 Size green paper. Please bring this printout to the college to continue with admission procedure.
